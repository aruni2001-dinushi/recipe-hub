Chocolate Lava Cake

Rich, decadent chocolate cakes with a warm, gooey center

Ingredients:

  • 4 ounces dark chocolate (60-70% cocoa)
  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Butter for ramekins
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Butter 4 ramekins and dust with cocoa powder.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter in a double boiler or microwave, stirring until smooth.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together powdered sugar, eggs, egg yolks, and vanilla.
  4. Slowly whisk the chocolate mixture into the egg mixture.
  5. Fold in flour and salt until just combined.
  6. Divide batter evenly among prepared ramekins.
  7. Bake for 12-14 minutes until edges are firm but centers are soft.
  8. Let cool for 1 minute, then invert onto plates and serve immediately with ice cream.

Prep time: 15 minutes | Cook time: 12-14 minutes

Classic New York Cheesecake

Creamy, rich cheesecake with a graham cracker crust

Ingredients:

For the Crust:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar

For the Filling:

  • 4 packages (8 oz each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1¼ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • ⅓ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup heavy cream

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C). Wrap a 9-inch springform pan with aluminum foil.
  2. Make the crust: Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar. Press firmly into the bottom of the pan.
  3. Bake crust for 10 minutes, then remove and cool.
  4. Make the filling: Beat cream cheese until smooth. Gradually add sugar and beat until fluffy.
  5. Mix in sour cream and vanilla. Add eggs one at a time, beating just until combined.
  6. Stir in flour and heavy cream until smooth (do not overmix).
  7. Pour filling over cooled crust and smooth the top.
  8. Place springform pan in a larger baking pan. Add hot water to come halfway up the sides.
  9. Bake for 1 hour 15 minutes until edges are set but center still jiggles slightly.
  10. Turn off oven, leave door slightly open, and let cheesecake cool in oven for 1 hour.
  11. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours (preferably overnight) before serving.

Prep time: 30 minutes | Cook time: 1 hour 15 minutes | Chill time: 4+ hours
